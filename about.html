Let`s Chat

Hello! I’m Lacie Dague Haarmann, a freelance graphic designer.

I’m a graphic designer and illustrator with over seven years of experience working in-studio and independently as a freelancer. I’ve helped build brand identities and promotional material for small businesses as well as marketing and advertisements, illustration, and digital products.

My love of art started at a young age, and I grew up drawing on any and every piece of paper that I could get my hands on. I first developed my skills in art in design at Eastern Illinois University, where I received my bachelor’s degree in art with a minor in marketing. I have since honed my design love for print material, and right after college I worked for a print shop where I learned the importance of setting up a file for print and printing a design on any surface.
